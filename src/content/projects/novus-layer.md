## The Challenge
Novus Layer had an innovative cloud security product but struggled with market penetration. Their previous website wasn't converting technical traffic, and their cost-per-acquisition (CPA) on Google Ads was unsustainable.

## Our Solution
We executed a complete digital transformation aimed at qualified lead generation.
1.  **Strategic Positioning:** Redefined their messaging to focus on ROI for C-level executives, not just features for developers.
2.  **Webflow Redesign:** Built a blazing-fast, dark-mode website focused on conversion funnels and clear calls-to-action.
3.  **Performance Marketing:** Restructured their Google and LinkedIn Ads accounts, focusing on high-intent keywords and retargeting sequences.

### Key Results
* **240% increase** in demo requests within 90 days.
* **55% decrease** in CPA across paid channels.
