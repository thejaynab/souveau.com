## The Challenge
Oura needed to launch into a saturated beauty market. They had great organic products but lacked a cohesive brand voice that could resonate with eco-conscious Millennial and Gen Z consumers.

## Our Solution
We built a brand rooted in transparency and nature.
1.  **Visual Identity:** Developed a minimalist, organic color palette and packaging design that stands out on Instagram.
2.  **E-commerce Build:** Created a custom Shopify theme emphasizing product ingredients and sustainability certifications.
3.  **Content Strategy:** Launched an influencer gifting program and an educational blog series on natural wellness, driving organic traffic before launch day.

### Key Results
* **Sold out inventory** in the first month of launch.
* Grew Instagram community to **15k active followers** organically.
