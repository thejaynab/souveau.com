## The Challenge
Karda's app had low visibility in the App Store and high churn rates during the onboarding process. They needed to lower their Cost Per Install (CPI) and improve retention.

## Our Solution
A dual-track approach focusing on acquisition and retention.
1.  **UX Audit:** Identified friction points in the sign-up flow and provided actionable redesign recommendations.
2.  **ASO Overhaul:** Rewrote titles, subtitles, and keyword fields based on competitive analysis, and designed new screenshot sets.
3.  **Paid Social:** Launched UGC-style ads on TikTok and Meta targeted at Gen Z users interested in financial literacy.

### Key Results
* **40% reduction** in Cost Per Install (CPI).
* **Top 10 ranking** in the Finance category within 3 months.
