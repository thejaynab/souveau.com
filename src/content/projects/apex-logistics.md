## The Challenge
Apex had a solid reputation but an outdated image that was hurting their ability to attract modern enterprise clients. Their sales process was entirely reliant on cold calling and referrals.

## Our Solution
We modernized their brand and automated their sales pipeline.
1.  **Visual Rebrand:** Created a strong, trustworthy new logo and visual identity system.
2.  **Website & Content:** Built a corporate site focusing on case studies and their technological capabilities.
3.  **LinkedIn Engine:** Optimized sales team profiles and implemented a Sales Navigator automation strategy to connect with supply chain directors.

### Key Results
* **15+ qualified sales meetings** generated per month via LinkedIn.
* Closed 2 major enterprise contracts within the first quarter of the new brand launch.
