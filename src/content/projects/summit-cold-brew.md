## The Challenge
Summit needed to break into the competitive RTD (Ready-to-Drink) coffee market. They needed a brand that felt adventurous and premium, not just another coffee brand.

## Our Solution
We positioned Summit as the fuel for outdoor adventurers.
1.  **Packaging:** Designed distinct, rugged can artwork that stands out on shelves and looks great in outdoor photography.
2.  **Launch Strategy:** coordinated a simultaneous launch across DTC e-commerce and select boutique retail partners.
3.  **Influencer Seeding:** Sent product kits to 200+ micro-influencers in the hiking, climbing, and van-life niches.

### Key Results
* Achieved nationwide retail distribution interest within 6 months.
* Generated over **1M impressions** on social media during launch week.
