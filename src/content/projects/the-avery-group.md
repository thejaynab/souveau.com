## The Challenge
The Avery Group was losing market share in the luxury condo sector to bigger, tech-savvy competitors. They were invisible on Google Maps for their most valuable target keywords.

## Our Solution
We implemented a hyper-local authority strategy.
1.  **Google Business Profile Optimization:** Fully optimized their profile and implemented a review generation strategy.
2.  **Local SEO:** Created neighborhood-specific landing pages targeting "luxury condos in [area]" and "penthouses for sale."
3.  **Video Production:** Produced high-end property tours and agent introduction videos to build trust before the first call.

### Key Results
* **Rank #1** on Google Maps for 5 major local keywords.
* **3x increase** in qualified inbound calls from the website.
